Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time with a 30-day notice. No long-term contracts required.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for new members to experience our facilities and services.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers. Monthly payments are automatically processed.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical reasons or travel.
