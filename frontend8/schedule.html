How to Book Classes

  1. Log into your PowerFit account
  2. Navigate to the schedule section
  3. Select your desired class and time
  4. Click "Book" to reserve your spot
  5. Receive confirmation via email

Class Policies

  • Arrive 10 minutes before class starts
  • Bring your own water bottle and towel
  • Wear appropriate workout attire
  • Cancel at least 2 hours before class
  • No-shows may result in booking restrictions
