Our Story

Founded in 2015, EcoGreen began with a simple mission: to make environmental protection accessible and effective for everyone. What started as a small team of passionate environmentalists has grown into a comprehensive environmental solutions company.

Today, we work with businesses, governments, and communities worldwide to implement sustainable practices and create lasting positive impact on our planet.
