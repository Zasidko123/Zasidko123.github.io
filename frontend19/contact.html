Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our travel services

How far in advance should I book my trip?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for international trips, especially during peak seasons. For popular destinations, booking 6-12 months ahead ensures the best availability and rates.

What's included in your travel packages?

Our packages typically include flights, accommodations, transfers, guided tours, and some meals. Each package is different, so we'll provide a detailed itinerary with all inclusions before booking.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we strongly recommend travel insurance for all international trips. We can arrange comprehensive coverage that includes medical, trip cancellation, and baggage protection.

Can you customize a package for my group?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom itineraries for groups of any size. Whether it's a family vacation, corporate retreat, or destination wedding, we'll design the perfect experience.
